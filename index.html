About


My name is Kelvin Wahome,I am a Full-Stack Web Developer.

Building state-of-the-art, user-friendly, easy to use websites and applications is truly a passion of mine.

I have a passion and keen eye for user-centered design, therefore, I always design my projects first before I start coding them.

Being a passionate individual, I view every experience as an opportunity to learn and better myself.Projects that challenge me to push myself beyond my comfort and boundaries are the best ones as I acquire new skills and knowledge while working on them.

I joined Moringa School in April 2017 where I enrolled for the 5-week Moringa Prep programme where I learnt HTML,CSS,Bootstrap and Javascript(Vanilla and Angular Javascript).After that I later joined the 15-week Moringa Core programme where I took a course in Python(Flask and Django frameworks).

Aside from coding,I also love watching and playing football and rugby and listening to music.
